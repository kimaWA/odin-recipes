Taco Casserole

Description

This tasty casserole recipe is considered the "best" taco casserole on the Allrecipes website.
My opinion? Well... you have to try it yourself!

Credits to "Allrecipes Test Kitchen" on Allrecipes.com, recipe submitted anonymously, for this delicious taco casserole dish.

Ingredients

The original recipe dish size will be included as measurements in the ingredient list below.

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 2 cups salsa
  • 1 (16 ounce) can chili beans, drained
  • 3 cups tortilla chips, crushed
  • 2 cups sour cream
  • 1 (2 ounce) can sliced black olives, drained
  • ½ cup chopped green onion
  • ½ cup chopped fresh tomato
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients and preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Spray a 9x13-baking dish with cooking spray.
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir ground beef until browned and crumbly, 8 to 10 minutes.
  3. Stir in your salsa, reduce heat, and simmer until liquid is absorbed, about 20 minutes.
    Stir in beans; cook until heated through.
  4. On the baking dish, spread your beef mixture over crushed tortilla chips on the bottom.
    Spread sour cream over the beef, then sprinkle olives, green onion, and tomatoes on top.
    Cover with Cheddar cheese.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until hot and bubbly, about 30 minutes.
  6. Serve with some chips and enjoy!
