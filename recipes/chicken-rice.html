Chicken and Rice

Description:

This tasty recipe of Mexican style chicken and rice is a favorite for any gym goer that requires a high protein, low calorie meal prep.

Credits to "CleanFoodRecipe" on Youtube for an easy to follow, cheap and delicious dish.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken Breast
  • Oregano
  • Garlic Powder
  • Paprika
  • Cumin
  • Chilli Powder
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Onion
  • Bell Pepper
  • Black Beans
  • Rice
  • Chopped Canned Tomatoes
  • Lime
  • Coriander

Steps:

The recipe below includes measurements for 3 servings when complete. Tweak to your liking.

  1. Add 750g of chicken breast into a dish and chop into cubes.
  2. Add 1 table spoon of your spices, beginning at oregano and ending at black pepper. Refer to the ingredient list above.
  3. Mix "till you lose patience," then cook for 6-8 minutes on medium heat (look for golden brown). Set aside.
  4. On a seperate plate, chop half of an onion and half of a bell pepper.
  5. In the same pan we cooked the chicken, add the onion and bell pepper.
  6. Cook for 2 minutes on medium heat before adding 100g of black beans, 210g of washed rice, and the same seasonings as before only just 1 tea spoon each.
  7. Let the ingredients in the pan toast for 1 minute before adding your can of chopped tomatoes and 300ml of water. Cover and simmer for 12-15 minutes after you see some bubbling.
  8. Once finished cooking, throw the rice mix with the chicken, minced coriandor, and a fully squeezed lime.
  9. Enjoy!

Nutrition Facts:

Per serving: 450 calories | 45g protein | 47g carbs | 9g fat | Tasty!!!
