Pasta Salad

Description

Pasta salad is a popular party dish that many families and invidiuals add to their catering, myself included.
Composed of an assortment of vegetables and rotini pasta topped with a homemade salad dressing, this recipe will not dissapoint!

Credits to "Allrecipes Test Kitchen" on Allrecipes.com, recipe submitted by Bonnies, for this famous dish.

Ingredients

The original recipe dish size will be included as measurements in the ingredient list below.

Pasta Salad:

  • 1 (8 ounce) package uncooked tri-color rotini pasta
  • 6 ounces pepperoni sausage, diced
  • 6 ounces provolone cheese, cubed
  • 1 medium red onion, very thinly sliced and cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 small cucumber, thinly sliced
  • ¾ cup chopped green bell pepper
  • ¾ cup chopped red bell pepper
  • 1 (6 ounce) can pitted black olives, drained
  • ¼ cup minced fresh parsley
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Homemade Salad Dressing:

  • ½ cup olive oil
  • ¼ cup red wine vinegar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • ½ teaspoon ground mustard seed
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

To Cook The Pasta:

Bring a large pot of slightly salted water to a boil. Add your rotini pasta and cook until tender
yet slightly firm to the bite, 8 to 10 minutes. Drain, rinse with cold water, and
drain again

To Make The Dressing:

Mix your olive oil, vinegar, garlic, basil, oregano, ground
mustard, salt, and pepper in a jar with a lid. Seal and shake until uniform.

  1. Transfer the cooked pasta to a large bowl. Add your pepperoni, provolone
    cheese, red onion, cucumber, bell peppers, olives, parsley, and Parmesan cheese.
  2. Pour the dressing over the pasta salad; toss until well coated. Serve immediately, or
    cover and chill in the refrigerator for up to 8 hours before serving.
  3. Enjoy!
